What is USCCA LaserTRAIN?

LaserTRAIN is high-volume, self-diagnostic training you can do in your own home. It is the perfect course for people looking to maximize their hands-on firearms training and minimize the costs of ammunition and wear on their pistol. This course will empower you to master the fundamentals of grip, presentation, trigger control, sight alignment and accurate shot placement with nearly unlimited training repetitions.

Advantages of LaserTRAIN

A SIRT pistol makes an excellent training tool because of its two primary features: a resetting trigger and dual lasers. The resetting trigger mimics the feel of a trigger on a real firearm. You can “shoot” and reset the trigger without having to manually rack the slide or cock a hammer. This allows you to make multiple shots without breaking your grip or losing your sight picture.
